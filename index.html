Presentation.js

HTML-Native
Presentations

A barebones, Unix-philosophy utility that turns pure HTML/CSS into a stateful presentation by toggling classes.

Press Space or Right Arrow to continue

Why use it?

Unlike heavy frameworks, you bring your own HTML and CSS. We add state.

No Virtual DOM

It's just vanilla JavaScript traversing and mutating standard DOM classes.

Bring Your Own CSS

Style with Tailwind, Bootstrap, or plain CSS. Use the visual tools you already know.

AI Friendly

LLMs are great at emitting styled HTML. Use them to generate slides instantly.

Simple Declarative Animations

Just add classes like 1--fade-in or 2--slide-up.

<div class="slide-base">
  <h2>Hello</h2>
  <!-- Appears on next click/step 1 -->
  <p class="hidden 1--visible">World</p>
</div>
